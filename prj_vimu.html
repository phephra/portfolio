visual museum

Visual Museum is a fictional Milan based museum of graphics and visual design, whose brand image was designed for the Visual Communication Design Studio at Politecnico di Milano. The project is tailored for an interactive, dynamic museum, that has a fresh and colourful approach to the discipline through vibrant colours and a bold appearance. A website and the image of an exhibition focused on book cover designer Chip Kidd, titled "Judge This!" were designed soon after, accompanied by a mobile app to enhance the visiting experience.

Together with: Edoardo Guido, Marcello Maffi, Lorenzo Marchesi, Luca Mazzeo
