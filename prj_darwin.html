Darwin Giramondo

Darwin Giramondo (in English "Darwin Globetrotter") is an illustrated sticker book on Darwin's voyage around the world, based on the first chapter of his book recalling the voyage of the Beagle. The project's aim was to translate Darwin's text in a saleable product. We decided to translate it in a sticker album for kids so that they could play learning something new about science, geography, history and art. The illustration style was inspired by Expressionist art and South African illustrator Maaikke Baaker. In particular, I created many of the illustrations for backgrounds and stickers, each on of them being a place visited by Darwin or a living being encountered in his journey.
