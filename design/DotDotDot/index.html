Dot Dot Dot magazine

2018       Editorial design & art direction

Dot Dot Dot was an iconic independent design journal of the past. During the Editorial Design Studio held by Massimo Pitis, the task was to to give the magazine a new flesh, designing a new fictional issue. The new Dot Dot Dot focuses on open contemporary culture, encompassing different narrative styles. With its content flowing like a musical composition, the first issue focuses on the theme "Revive". A transmedia extension of the printed magazine, Dot Dot Dot Radio, was also created.
