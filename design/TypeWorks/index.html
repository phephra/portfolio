Selected Type Works

This a selection of works created during the Advanced Typography course at Lahti University of Applied Sciences. The aim of the classes was to produce short assignments, created in 24 hours, playing with typography in an advanced way - experimenting with styles and at the same time focusing on its neat usage in more classical solutions. All the posters are A4 size.
