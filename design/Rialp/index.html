Alessandro Ferrari

Rialp

2021       Design system

Rialp is a Spanish publishing house, its catalogue focusing mainly on poetry, religion and non-fiction. In 150UP, I worked on Rialp’s rebranding, by setting the main rules of the brand identity system, creating the new brand guidelines and identity implementation – setting a new palette with vivid and bright colours and revisiting the book covers and typography.
