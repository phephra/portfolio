Urban Hive Milano

2022       Graphic Design & Animation

The Session is a fictional digital magazine for iPad created as a demonstration for my students at the Computer Graphic 2 – InDesign class at IED. Aimed at music geeks and connosseurs, the magazine adapts articles found on Red Bull Music Academy, the issue at hand focusing on influential Japanese musicians.
