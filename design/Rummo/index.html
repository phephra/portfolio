Rummo

2023-2024       Art direction, Graphic design & Animation

During the second half of 2023 and the beginning of 2024, in 150UP I had the pleasure of supervising the art direction of Rummo's social media contents. The challenge was to channel Rummo's timeless and elegant brand image on the monthly editorial plan, pushing the content at the foreground. Particular care was taken in the usage of typography.
