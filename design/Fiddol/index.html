Fiddol

Fiddol is a service for kindergarten kids that aims to make the brushing of teeth a daily routine, both at home and at school, through music and interactive storytelling. Created during the Interaction Design Studio after preliminary user research, Fiddol blends together music, narration and dental care education, thanks to a smart toy synchronized with a mobile application. A proof of concept prototype was created using Raspberry Pi Zero, Python and Pure Data. The real scale demonstrative prototype was 3D printed.
