MOAK:
Coffee Jockey

2021       UX/UI & Illustration

Coffee Jockey is an innovative coffee machine of MOAK’s For You system, designed by product designer Odo Fioravanti. In 150UP I designed the user interface of the interactive display, providing colourful isometric illustrations reminiscent of an aesthetic that nobody would ever dare to associate with coffee-making – the one of contemporary isometric indie-games.
